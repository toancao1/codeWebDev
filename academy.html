Jedi Training Academy

The Jedi Training Academy offers guests of any age the chance to train in the way of the Jedi.

Our training sessions are according to level:

  1. Younglings: For those who have yet to take the Initiate Trials.
  2. Padawan: For those who’ve completed the Initiate Trials.
  3. Jedi Knight: For those who’ve completed the Jedi trials.
  4. Jedi Master: For those who are members of the Order.

A short test will be given at the beginning of your first session to gauge your level.

Events:

  • Once a month our Jedi Training Academy hosts a Defecting to the Dark Side event!

Please see our Ticket page for information on pricing.
