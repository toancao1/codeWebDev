I. How much do parking permits cost and where can I park?

  • Permits for cars and other grounded vehicles:
    • $7 per Hour.
    • $35 for a day pass.
  • Special parking permits for spaceships:1
    • $10 per Hour.
    • $50 for a day pass.

  • For parking locations please visit our Map.

II. I've lost something, now what?

  • The Edge of the Galaxy Park is dedicated to giving our guests a lasting experience of wonder and adventure.
  • If a lost item is found on park premise or by our staff member at our off planet attractions, the item will be set aside for 5 months time in a secure location.
  • Please contact the customer service office at 1(800)-666 Lost for more details.

III. Are pets allowed in the park?

  • No living pets or droids allowed on park premise!
  • Exception will be made for service animals or droids with valid permits. Please call our customer service office at 1(800)-666 Lost for more details.

IV. How accessible is the Edge of the Galaxy Park?

  • The Edge of the Galaxy Park is accessible for all!
  • We welcome guests from around the globe and across the universe!
  • Please call our customer service office at 1(800)-666 Lost if special needs must be taken into consideration.

V. How do I get to an attraction?

  • The Edge of the Galaxy Park is pleased to provide guests without our Spaceship shuttle service!
  • Shuttles travel back and forth between the park's off planet attractions and their respective on planet park buildings every 15 minutes.
  • Exact shuttle times and schedules are available around the park at various locations.

VI. How much are tickets for the Jedi Academy?

  • The Jedi Academy is an add on to a regular admissions ticker.
  • The following fee is required for entrance to the Jedi Training Academy:+$20 for any regular admission, +$15 for any family admission, and +$10 for any children admission.
